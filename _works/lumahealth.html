Luma Health

Luma Health is a Patient Success Platform to unify and automate patient journeys for health systems, FQHCs, & specialty practices.

I worked on multiple teams and initiatives at Luma including our solutions for Telehealth, Financial Journey, Design System and product Dashboard.

While at Luma I contributed or led the following initiatives:

  1. Collaborated with engineering, design and product to build and mature a Design System
  2. Redesigned our telehealth product to be more performant, accessible and inclusive
  3. Led the design of (while also collaborating with a junior designer) two net-new financial journey products: LumaPay and Insurance Verification
  4. Mentored multiple fellow designers (with plans to be promoted to Director of Design)
