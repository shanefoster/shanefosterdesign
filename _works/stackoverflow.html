Stack Overflow

I'm guessing you already know what Stack Overflow is, but if you happen to not, here's a brief description:

Stack Overflow is the largest, most trusted online community for developers to learn, share their programming knowledge, and build their careers.

While at Stack Overflow I was the lead designer for our Stacks Design System. I worked directly with an amazing team of three engineers and I loved every second of it. I also consistenly collaborated with the many stacks stakeholders as well as a design team of 15.

While at Stack Overflow I contributed or led the following initiatives:

Led and conducted all interviews and research for the system, making sure we focus on consumer pain points and making the greatest impact possible.

Define a design system governance process resulting in less friction and an increase in contributions to the system.

Refactored and optimized nearly 100% of UI kit components resulting in increased designer efficiency and accelerated time to release.

Created and implemented a strategy to introduce design tokens, in attempt to bridge the gap between deisgn and engineering and increase consistency across the system.

Created new Design Guidelines documentation that were abstracted out from each Stacks component implementation strategy resulting in a better understanding of how, when and why to use a certain component.
