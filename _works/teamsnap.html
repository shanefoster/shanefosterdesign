TEAM MANAGEMENT, MADE EASY

I first used TeamSnap in 2006 and instantly fell in love with it.

WINTER IS COMING

Okay, maybe not Winter, but many huuuuuuge design upates are. When I arrived at TeamSnap, the design team was in its infancy. It was an exciting time to join. Mobile applications didn't yet exist. The web app hadn't had any more major design updates (UX or Visual) in almost a decade. As a small team, we got to come together and build a new design system from the ground up.

TIME FOR A FACELIFT

We created mood boards, brainstormed typography, designed style tiles and got to specify the emotion and tone we wanted to portray to our users when using our products. I collaborated with another designer on the complete visual redesign of the web app. Once we agreed on our visual language, we started to build a modular, component-based design system in Sketch as well as a hosted pattern library for the development team to access.
